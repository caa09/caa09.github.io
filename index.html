Welcome to

Heriot-Watt's women@CS

Heriot-Watt women@CS is a group formed of Postgraduate and Undergraduate females studying computer science related degrees at Heriot-Watt University.

The student-led group holds monthly events and offers the opportunity to attend industry talks while also allowing you to network with other females in CS at Heriot-Watt through Discord.
