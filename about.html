women@CS

At Heriot-Watt there are a number of initatives for females in STEM to help them create connections and feel more comfortable in such a male-dominated industry.

women@CS is designed specifically for females studying Computer Science and aims to create a community amongst women in CS degrees and give them a chance to socialise, attend events and get advice from other female students.

women@CS was incredibly active - and popular - before the pandemic and the lack of in-person university teaching has fueled the need for it to be restarted to help those in early years of their degree to feel confident and included.

Values

Community

women@CS wishes to create a community amongst females in computer science

Inclusive

everyone is welcome and they will always be treated equally

Support

get support from others and be guided on where a CS degree can take you

Fun

most of all, women@CS is a fun and lighthearted place where females can have fun and enjoy themselves
